How do I get into my personal Kindful account to enter a pledge?

What paperwork do I need to give to a business if they plan on donating a large monetary lump sum?
If they donate via Kindful, they will receive their donation letter immediately. If they donate via check, they won't receive their donation letter until the last week of January. Kindful, Donations, Business Partner

How can I ensure that the Kindful money goes to the designated Life Changing Gift(s)?
BCE directly pays for the Life Changing Gift(s). For example, if the Life Changing Gift is a rent/mortgage payment, you would need to send a copy of their bill/invoice directly to BCE. The funds are not simply disbursed to the family to spend as they want. Kindful, Life Changing Gift

How are checks handled?
The donor should write the check out to Best Christmas Ever. It should be mailed to BCE PO Box 439. Cloquet, MN 55720 by either the donor or the Captain. Have the donor include their email address (so BCE can email them a donation slip) and they include the Captain's name or Team name. Captains can manually input the donation into their Kindful account, or have Stephanie do it. If the Captains do it themselves, they should let Stephanie know so that the money doesn't get "double-dipped." Kindful, Donations
